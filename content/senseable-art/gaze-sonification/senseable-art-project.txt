Title: Gaze Sonification

----

Subtitle: Mediation artifact based on Sophie Taeuber-Arp’s ‘Composition Horizontale-Verticale’ (LAC Collection)

----

Authors: Selena Savic / Gabriele Gambotto / Peter Law

----

Abstract: The concept of the project is to create an interactive sonification of Sophie Taeuber-Arp’s ‘Composition Horizontale-Verticale’ for all visitors (blind, partially-sighted and sighted).
